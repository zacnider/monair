Interactive Monad Testnet Visualizer

🌟 What is this project?

This is an interactive visualization of the Monad Testnet blockchain featuring the magical theme of Cappadocia. Watch real blockchain transactions come to life as cute creatures called Monanimals board hot air balloons representing blockchain blocks!

🐠 What are Monanimals?

Monanimals are adorable creatures that represent individual transactions on the Monad blockchain. Each Monanimal has a unique appearance based on its transaction type:

Transfer
Approve
Swap
Stake
Unstake
Claim
Contract

🎈 What are the Balloons?

Hot air balloons represent blockchain blocks. Each balloon:

  • 🏷️ Shows the block number and transaction count
  • ⏳ Waits for Monanimals (transactions) to board
  • 🚀 Launches when full or after a timeout
  • ✨ Flies away, confirming the block on the blockchain

🎮 How to Use

1
Watch the Magic

Monanimals spawn automatically and walk to balloons representing real Monad testnet blocks

2
Click Monanimals

Click any Monanimal to see detailed transaction information including hash, addresses, and gas fees

3
Click Balloons

Click balloons to see block details and all transactions within that block

4
Explore Features

Use controls to add Monanimals, change weather, switch between balloons/zeppelins, and toggle dark mode

🔧 Features

  • 🌐 Real-time blockchain data from Monad Testnet
  • 🎨 7 unique Monanimal types for different transaction types
  • 🌤️ Dynamic weather system affecting balloon movement
  • 🎵 Background music with volume control
  • 🌙 Dark/Light mode with stars and moon
  • 🎈 Balloon/Zeppelin modes for different flight styles
  • 📊 Real-time network stats (TPS, gas prices, block height)
  • 🔍 Blockchain explorer integration for detailed transaction viewing

🌐 About Monad

Monad is a high-performance blockchain designed for scalability and speed. This visualizer connects to the Monad Testnet to show real blockchain activity in an engaging, educational way.
